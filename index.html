Why should you love killer whales?

Killer whales are the top predators of the sea. They live in family groups called pods and eat fish, sharks, and marine mammals.

Why are killer whales in groups?

Killer whales are in groups called "pods" which are family units that can be large or small.

What do they eat?

An orca's diet varies, but they can eat fish, sharks, and mammals like whales, seals, and sea lions.

Why are they black and white?

Orcas are black and white to blend in with their ocean surroundings.
